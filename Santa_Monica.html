Santa Monica

The first group of people to explore what is known as Santa Monica were the Tongva tribe. Spanish travelers would later discover the area of Santa Monica during the time missions were being built along the coast. A path would be created that would connect these missions and pass by Santa Monica which is known as The Camino Real. In 1872 the land would be purchased and founded by Colonel Baker who traveled from the east coast.


Santa Monica would soon be turned into a popular tourist location as their famous pier recieves 10 million visitors year round. If you enjoy going to beach and being outdoors, Santa Monica is the perfect place since their beach is 3 miles long. Whether you want to sunbathe, surf, or play volleybal, Santa Monica beach is a great place to do all of these activies and more. The Santa Monica located at Santa Monica beach has an entire theme park with rollercoasters,games,and stores to shop around.
