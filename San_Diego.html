San Diego

The first people to explore the area of the San Diego, were the native American tribe known as the Kumeyaay tribe. In the year 1512 the Spanish would take over the land and name the land San Miguel and it wouldnt get its current name in 1602.

San Diego would grow into one of the most interesting cities in California. The location of San Diego near the coast and along the border of Mexico made it a more popular destination for beach lovers and passengers trying to get into Mexico. San Diego is also known for their great weather as it seems to be always sunny and never rainy. One of the most interesting places to visit within the city is Legoland, which is a themepark dedicated to lego's.
